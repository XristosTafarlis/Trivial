How to Play

Welcome to QUIZ DAYS AND NIGHTS!

To start the game kindly select number of players or player teams (from 2 up to 4), select the playersâ€™ names and their respective avatars. The order of the players here will define the order of playing the game among players. Once ready you may start the game by clicking on the Play button.

The game begins and all the avatars are at the bottom left corner. Player 1 should select a corner on the board and decide/select if he wants to answer an easy, medium or hard question. If he answers correctly, he gets 1,2, or 3 points respectively to the level of difficulty he chose and then he may roll the dice, move according steps on the boards, and continue playing till he answers wrong.

Then the 2nd player will start from another corner of the board and move with the approach described above. All players can begin the game only from different corners of the board. They can move only clockwise. If a player has to move to a position where another player already is, according to the dice roll he achieved, then he loses his turn as this is not possible. The game ends when a player has gathered 10 points.

Enjoy QUIZ DAYS AND NIGHTS!
